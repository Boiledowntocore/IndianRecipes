ALOO PARATHA!

    Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 2 medium-sized potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil
  • Water as needed

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour and a pinch of salt. Gradually add water to make a soft dough. Cover it and let it rest for 15-20 minutes.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the mashed potatoes, chopped onion, green chilies, cumin seeds, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt to make the filling.
  3. Divide the dough into small equal-sized balls. Roll out each ball into a small circle.
  4. Place a portion of the potato filling in the center of each circle.
  5. Bring the edges of the circle together to enclose the filling and form a stuffed ball.
  6. Flatten the stuffed ball with your hands and gently roll it out into a paratha (flatbread) using a rolling pin.
  7. Heat a griddle (tava) and cook the rolled paratha on both sides until it turns golden brown, applying ghee or oil as needed.
  8. Repeat the process for the remaining dough balls and filling.
  9. Your delicious Aloo Parathas are ready to be served hot with yogurt, pickle, or chutney!
