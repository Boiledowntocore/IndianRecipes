CHAAT MASALA!

Ingredients:

For Boiling Sweet Potato

  • Oil for frying, तेल तलने के लिए
  • 4 medium Sweet potato, शकरकंद
  • Salt to taste, नमक स्वादअनुसार
  • 1 tsp Sugar, चीनी​​
  • Water, पानी

    • Ingredients:

  • In a kadai, add ghee, once it melts, add dry red chillies, cumin seeds, fennel seeds, onion seeds, cloves and let it splutter well.
  • Add ginger, green tomatoes, salt to taste, jaggery, water as required,cover it with the lid and cook it for 4-5 minutes or until cooked.
  • Add vinegar, degi red chili powder and mix it well.
  • In a kadai, add ghee, once its melt, add sweet potato and fry well.
  • In a serving plate, add curd, prepared chutney, fried sweet potato, lotus root chips, fried fox nut, sprinkle some chaat masala, pomegranate pearl, coriander sprig and serve.
